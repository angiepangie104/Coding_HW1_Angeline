Our Mission
Our mission is to advocate for accountability of the Oakland Police Department to the community so that the Oakland Police Department operates with equitable, just, constitutional, transparent policies and practices that reflect the values and engender the trust of the community.

What we've been up to
This past year, our Coalition engaged in extensive outreach efforts aimed at bringing the voices of those most impacted by OPD to the attention of the Police Commission, strengthening the Commission’s ability to enact effective reform and increasing community awareness of the Commission.
